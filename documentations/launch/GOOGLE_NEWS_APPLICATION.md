# Google News Publisher Center — Application Checklist

**Apply at:** https://publishercenter.google.com

## Prerequisites (all done)

- [x] Live website at `coindaily.online`
- [x] Google News XML sitemap at `/api/sitemap/news` (last 2 days of articles)
- [x] Article structured data (JSON-LD `NewsArticle` schema)
- [x] Unique, original content (AI-generated but human-reviewed)
- [x] Clear author attribution on articles
- [x] Published date + updated date on all articles
- [x] About page at `/about`
- [x] Editorial Standards page at `/editorial-standards`
- [x] Contact information (editorial@coindaily.online)
- [x] Privacy Policy at `/privacy`
- [x] Terms of Service at `/terms`

## Application Steps (Day 1)

1. **Go to** https://publishercenter.google.com
2. **Add publication:**
   - Publication name: `CoinDaily Africa`
   - Website URL: `https://coindaily.online`
   - Language: English (primary)
   - Country: Nigeria (primary market)
3. **Content settings:**
   - Content type: News
   - Topics: Business, Finance, Cryptocurrency, Technology
   - Section URLs:
     - `/ng/news/` (Nigeria)
     - `/ke/news/` (Kenya)
     - `/za/news/` (South Africa)
     - `/gh/news/` (Ghana)
4. **Feed configuration:**
   - RSS feed: `https://coindaily.online/api/rss` (if exists)
   - News sitemap: `https://api.coindaily.online/api/sitemap/news`
5. **Visual branding:**
   - Upload logo (SVG or PNG, min 1000x1000)
   - Set brand colors: `#f97316` (orange)
6. **Submit for review**

## Expected Timeline

- Review: 2-4 weeks
- Approval rate for new publishers: ~40%
- Key factors: original content, editorial standards, regular publishing cadence

## Post-Approval

- Articles appear in Google News within minutes of publishing
- Monitor Google Search Console → Performance → News tab
- Maintain at least 3 articles/day publishing cadence
- Monitor for manual actions in Search Console

## Rejection Reasons (avoid these)

- Thin/duplicated content
- No clear editorial standards
- No author/publisher attribution
- Misleading headlines
- Excessive ads above the fold
- Content behind hard paywall (soft paywall OK)
